In his spare time, Russell enjoys developing Android applications using both native and web technologies. He hopes to extend his knowledge to build on the iOS platform in the near future.

Oftentimes we find ourselves in a dilemma of not knowing what movie to watch. Especially useful in group settings, Watch This Movie uses The Movie Database (tmdb) API to sort the most recent films and DVD releases by rating, box office, release date, or popularity. It allows the user to choose up to four potential movies and between those options it randomly recommends which one to watch. Eliminate the decision making process and allow this app to dictate your next movie night.

(IN PROGRESS) Combining elements of Taboo and Charades, Salad Bowl brings the popular party game on the go. Normally conducted with players drawing words scribbled on pieces of paper from a bowl, hence the name of the game, this app removes the need for physical materials and allows users to play whenever and wherever. The mobile version includes a built-in timer, scorekeeper, and a user-generated word bank.
